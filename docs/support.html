FAQ

I don't feel anything during the hold. That's normal — relax and breathe naturally. Never practice breath holds in water, while driving, or standing up.

The app has no sound. Check the volume sliders in Settings and your device's silent switch.

Where is my data? Nowhere but your device. Тиша collects nothing — see our Privacy Policy.
